FAQ

Questions, answered

How long does a website take to build?

Most projects launch within 1–3 weeks depending on scope. Basic sites can be ready in as little as 7 days, while larger e-commerce or custom builds take a little longer.

Do you design and develop, or just one?

Both. I handle the entire process end-to-end — strategy, UI/UX design, and full development — so you get one consistent point of contact and a cohesive result.

Will my website work on mobile?

Always. Every site I build is mobile-first and tested across phones, tablets, and desktops to look and perform flawlessly everywhere.

Can you update an existing website?

Yes. Whether it's a refresh, a redesign, or new features, I can work with your current site or rebuild it from the ground up — whichever serves your goals best.

Do you offer ongoing support?

I do. Premium projects include 30 days of support, and I offer flexible monthly maintenance plans for updates, backups, and peace of mind.

How do payments work?

Typically 50% upfront to reserve your slot and 50% on completion before launch. Larger projects can be split into milestones — just ask.
